Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate of Vision Group in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences . I got a B.E. degree in Tianjin University, Tianjin in June 2017.

My research interests include computer vision and deep learning, specifically for object sensing, weakly supervision and data engineering.
